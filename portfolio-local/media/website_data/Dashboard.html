OUTPUT_HERE

Body Age

OUTPUT_HERE

Body Age is calculated based on Basal metabolism. The age of the body is a measure of the physical condition of a person who is above and below the actual age. It is the criterion for judging the condition of the body. Two persons height and weight may be the same, but because of differences in body fat and basal metablism, it leads differences in body Age.
